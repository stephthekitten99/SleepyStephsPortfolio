Serenity

Its hard to find something that allows us to be centered in our own versions of peace. Futhermore, as a child it is really hard to recieve validation for the many stressers they go through throughout their adolence. Growing up it was always a common theme that when I or a friend would go to their parents about a problem the issue with either chopped up to be no big deal or they would say the famous line. "Well you don't have to work or pay bills so get over it." This statement always caused more stress and aggrevation in the long run. In this image I really wanted to capture the sense of peace that adolence with a healthy way of coping with stress can have.


Model: Hailey Lee (AKA My younger sister)
