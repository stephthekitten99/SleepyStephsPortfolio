Hot Garbo

In class we were asked to make a meme about something that has a meaning to us in our current lives. Nothing stood out to me more than my childhood favorite character Winnie The Pooh and my consistent obsession to get good grades. Unfortunately, since classes went to full online I had really struggled with my grades due to poor mental health. So I wanted to make lite out of the situation by making fun of myself. I felt that the fear of judgement every student goes through sharing their grades with others was extremely relatable and honestly kinda funny.
