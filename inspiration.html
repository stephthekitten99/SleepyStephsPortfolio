My Favorite Artists

All of these are Huge Inspirations to my Personal Art

Please Feel Free to check out these artists they are all amazing in their own way!








Marchu

Marchu is an artist I met through xXScreamkiwiXx on twitch. So is super sweet and her art is awesome!

Ariimaw

I found Ariimaw on instagram and have been following her for years. Her kawaii aesthetic drawings not onlt give me inspiration, but often get me back into drawing.

Charlotte Ann Harris

Charlotte Ann Harris is a tattoo artist, being so her art is not only bold. It is super clean and really appealing to the eye.

Jaalidraws

Once more we have another kawaii based artist. This time we have Jaalidraws, one of my favorite things about her Works are the attention to detail in her eyes specifically.

Kumaartsu

Kuma is not only an awesome artist, but also a streamer. I first found her through a giveaway and we have talked ever since. Each of her characters is extremely unique and I love the distinct personallity they all have.
