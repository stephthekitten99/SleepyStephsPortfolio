Screamkiwi Vector

In creating this piece I was using it as an oppurtunity to get familiar with Procreate. I had never used it before outside of a handwriting practice environment. This piece is of a victor image mock up of really amazing Twitch streamer called xXScreamKiwiXx. Wiwi as all of her friends and fans call her has been a huge inspriation to me since my Freshman year of Highschool (7 years). She has taught me that being yourself can be really awesome. Especially getting to express yourself outwardly. Without her I would still be an extremely shy person, afraid to show others my true self. Though she is just a streamer and I have not ever met her she has made a huge impact on my lifestyle by showing me that perserverance is the key to a happy career.
