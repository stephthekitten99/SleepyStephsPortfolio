Gamer Girl Problems


In this video we were challenged to talk about Utopia/Dystopia, the topic that I felt represented this the best was the treatment female gamers recieve in our gaming community. In this most, of my personal experience with male gamers have been negative. I really wanted the audience to become more aware of this issue, as well as, for my female audience to know they are not alone.
